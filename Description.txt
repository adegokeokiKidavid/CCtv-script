To use it you will have to modify three lines
1. firstly modify 'line 13' in the "EmailToUse@gmail.com" by inputing the email you
 want to send from
2. Secondly modify 'line 14' in the "youremail@gmail.com" to your phones/devices email
inwhich you want to recieve the alert of motion detected
3. Thirdly modify 'line 15' "sender_email_password" to the "EmailToUse@gmail.com"
 line 13 email password

4. Install the requirements.txt by typing 'pip install -r requirements.txt'
It is advisable to create new emails for this to work perfectly
